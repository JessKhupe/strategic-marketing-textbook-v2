# Chapter 15: Ethics, Governance & Responsible Marketing

!!! note "Bloom's Level & Duration"
    **Bloom's Level:** Evaluate, Create  
    **Duration:** 2 weeks

## Summary

Examines legal, ethical, and governance structures required to deploy AI in marketing responsibly: transparency, fairness, accountability, and compliance.

## Learning Objectives

1. Draft a responsible AI policy for marketing teams.  
2. Identify bias and develop mitigation strategies.  
3. Establish KPIs for fairness, privacy, and transparency.

## Core Concepts

- Model transparency and explainability  
- Regulatory considerations (GDPR, CCPA, ad disclosures)  
- Organizational governance and review boards  

## Exercises

1. Perform a bias audit on a prediction model and propose fixes.  
2. Draft an AI marketing policy and approval workflow.
