# Chapter 14: Growth, Retention & Lifecycle Marketing

!!! note "Bloom's Level & Duration"
    **Bloom's Level:** Analyze, Evaluate  
    **Duration:** 2 weeks

## Summary

Focuses on activation funnels, retention loops, value-based segmentation, and product-led growth techniques supported by AI-driven personalization.

## Learning Objectives

1. Design lifecycle funnels and retention experiments.  
2. Build churn prediction models and retention playbooks.  
3. Implement value-based upsell and cross-sell strategies.

## Topics

- Activation, engagement, and retention frameworks
- Churn modeling and intervention design
- Loyalty program design and measurement

## Tools

| Tool | Use case |
|---|---|
| Amplitude / Mixpanel | Behavioral analytics |
| Braze / Customer.io | Lifecycle orchestration |

## Project

Students design a retention experiment with a machine-learned churn model and multi-armed treatment variants.
