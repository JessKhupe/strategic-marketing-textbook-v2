# Chapter 12: Performance Marketing & Measurement

!!! note "Bloom's Level & Duration"
    **Bloom's Level:** Apply, Analyze  
    **Duration:** 2–3 weeks

## Summary

This chapter focuses on performance-driven paid media: search, social, programmatic, and DTC channels optimized by data. Topics include budget allocation, targeting, bid strategies, incrementality testing, and automation. Students learn the math and psychology of profitable scaling.

## Learning Objectives

1. Plan full-funnel paid media programs with CPA/CAC targets aligned to unit economics.  
2. Design and execute incrementality tests (holdout, geo-lift, time-series experiments).  
3. Instrument events and use event-driven analytics for real-time optimization.  
4. Build automated bid strategies with safety guardrails.
