# Chapter 10: Accessibility & Inclusive Marketing

!!! note "Bloom's Level & Duration"
    **Bloom's Level:** Apply, Analyze  
    **Duration:** 2 weeks

## Summary

Accessibility is reframed as strategic market expansion. Students learn WCAG standards, inclusive content design, and representation considerations for AI imagery.

## Learning Objectives

1. Audit marketing assets against WCAG 2.1 standards.  
2. Design inclusive content systems using AI captioning and alt‑text generation.  
3. Evaluate representation in AI‑generated imagery.  
4. Build measurement frameworks tracking reach across audience segments.

## Core Theory

- WCAG 2.1 standards and conformance levels
- Universal design principles
- Alt‑text and image description best practices
- Inclusive language and representation
- Color contrast and cognitive accessibility

## Frameworks

| Framework | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Inclusive Marketing Audit | Checklist for accessibility and representation across assets |
| Accessibility ROI Calculator | Model business impact of accessibility improvements |
| Representation Checklist | Ensure diverse and non‑stereotypical depictions in imagery |

## AI Tool Applications

| Tool | Use case |
|---|---|
| Microsoft Accessibility Checker | Asset audits and suggestions |
| Google Lighthouse | Web accessibility scoring |
| Otter.ai / Be My Eyes AI | Automated captioning and assistance tools |

## Ethical Checkpoints

!!! warning "Ethical Checkpoints"
    - **Tokenism vs inclusion:** Strive for meaningful representation rather than box‑checking.  
    - **AI stereotype risk:** Monitor generators for biased outputs.  
    - **Pricing equity:** Avoid excluding groups with inaccessible pricing or interfaces.

## MicroSim: The Inclusive Campaign Audit

Students audit a campaign, redesign key assets for accessibility, and present an inclusion roadmap with cost/benefit analysis.

## Case Studies

- **Microsoft:** inclusive design practices.  
- **Target:** adaptive clothing launch and inclusive marketing.  
- **Airbnb:** accessibility features mainstreamed.
